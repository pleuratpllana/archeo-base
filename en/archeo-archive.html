Scanning Documents

Digitizing documents allows data to be stored in a dematerialized manner, easily accessed and most importantly, fully exploited.

The content of digitized typed reports can be easily read and exploited by analytical tools. ArcheoArchive will rediscover hidden information and link it to other information.

Digital data sustainability

Archives require electronic formats to be durable in time. Regular updates to file formats are required to ensure long-term readability.

The ArcheoArchive module will automatically update file formats to ensure that documents published in older formats can remain searchable over time.
